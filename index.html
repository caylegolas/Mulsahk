About Mulsahk

Mulsahk is a conlang (constructed language) developed by Cayden Rogers. This website is dedicated to exploring the alphabet, pronunciation, and unique aspects of the Mulsahk language. The font reflects handwritten glyphs created specifically for Mulsahk.
